## 🔹 Overview
**KrishiOS** is an **AI-powered agriculture management platform** designed to help farmers manage **crops, livestock, and marketplace activities** through a single digital system.  

The platform provides **AI-based recommendations, disease detection, and farm management tools** through a simple **Bangla-first interface**, making modern agricultural technology accessible to rural farmers.  

---

## 🎯 Problem Addressed
- Many farmers lack access to modern agricultural technology and expert guidance.  
- Inefficient farm management leads to **lower productivity** and **higher costs**.  

---

## 💡 Solution & Features
### 1️⃣ AI-Powered Crop & Livestock Management
- Intelligent decision support for **crop planning** and **disease detection**  
- Livestock monitoring and management tools to improve health and productivity  

### 2️⃣ Marketplace Integration
- Connect farmers with marketplaces to **sell products efficiently**  
- Track pricing, demand, and supply trends  

### 3️⃣ Bangla-First User Interface
- Simplified UI in **Bangla language** for easy access by rural farmers  
- Reduces technological barriers and improves adoption  

### 4️⃣ Productivity & Cost Optimization
- AI-driven insights help farmers **increase yields** and **reduce costs**  
- Offers actionable recommendations for day-to-day farming operations
